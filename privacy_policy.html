Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Umrah AI is published by Ali Tumkaya. Privacy requests may be sent to support.umrahai@gmail.com.

Data We Process

Depending on the features used and permissions granted, Umrah AI may process account and session data, app preferences and progress, location for prayer times, nearby places and routing, messages entered into the Islamic guide, technical request data, diagnostics, and advertising data.

Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, IP address, diagnostic information, ad interactions, and approximate location derived from the IP address to deliver, measure, secure, and personalize ads where consent has been given.

Purposes and Storage

Data is used to provide app functionality, remember preferences, calculate prayer times, show maps and routes, provide Quran and guide content, deliver notifications, support users, secure the service, and display and measure advertising. Local accounts and preferences are stored on the device. Passwords are not stored in plain text.

Third-Party Services

Depending on the feature used, the app may connect over HTTPS to services including CARTO or Mapbox, Overpass API, Aladhan, AlQuran Cloud, Google services, Google AdMob and its advertising partners, Sentry, payment services, and operating-system location or geocoding services. These providers process data under their own policies. Umrah AI does not sell personal data.

Advertising and Consent

Umrah AI uses Google AdMob. Where required, Google's User Messaging Platform presents consent choices before ads are requested. Users can review or withdraw advertising consent at any time from More > Privacy options in the app. Withdrawing consent may result in non-personalized or limited ads where permitted. See Google's advertising data practices.

Location and Notifications

Location is requested only for location-based features and can be denied or revoked in system settings. Local reminders are scheduled only when enabled by the user and notification permission is granted.

Deletion

Users can clear local account data through More > Delete Account. See the account and data deletion page, or email support.

Children and International Transfers

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. Some service providers may operate outside the user's country and process data under applicable safeguards.

Changes

Material changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
