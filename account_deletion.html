Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Delete Inside the App

  1. Open Umrah AI.
  2. Tap More.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

This clears the local account record, session, preferences, saved progress, bookmarks, counters, and other app data stored on that device.

Request Deletion Outside the App

Email support.umrahai@gmail.com from the address used in the app, or provide enough information to verify the request. Requests are reviewed within the applicable legal period; Turkish KVKK requests are targeted within 30 days after identity verification.

Data that was never sent from the device has no remote copy for Umrah AI to delete. Third-party providers may retain their own records under their policies.
